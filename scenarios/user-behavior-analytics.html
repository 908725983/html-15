Core Workflows

Five core user behavior analytics workflows

Workflow 01 · Most Powerful

Behavioral Retention Analysis — What Predicts Stickiness?

Ask: For users who signed up in Q1, which specific actions in the first 7 days most strongly predict 90-day retention? Show me the retention rate for users who performed each action vs. those who didn't. Include acquisition channel and account tier as context. InfiniSynapse joins product usage data with CRM and marketing attribution — revealing the behavioral patterns that predict retention, not just the actions that happened. The analysis that would take a data analyst a week is done in 2 minutes.

Workflow 02

Funnel Drop-Off Root Cause

Ask: Where are users dropping off in the signup-to-activation funnel? What distinguishes users who complete activation from those who don't — across product actions, acquisition source, and device type?

Workflow 03

Behavioral User Segmentation

Ask: Segment users into power users, casual users, and at-risk users based on behavior patterns. For each segment, show acquisition channel mix, feature usage profile, and 90-day retention rate.

Workflow 04

Churn Early Warning System

Ask: Identify users whose behavior in the last 14 days matches the pre-churn pattern of users who churned in the last 6 months. Flag them with churn probability and recommended intervention.

Workflow 05

Feature Adoption & Impact Analysis

Ask: For users who adopted Feature X, how does their 60-day retention compare to matched users who didn't? Break down by acquisition channel and account tier.

When InfiniSynapse is not the right choice for user behavior analytics

  • Your behavioral questions are purely in-product. If all your questions are "which features do users use most?" and you're happy with Amplitude/Mixpanel, dedicated product analytics tools are purpose-built for that scope.
  • You have a data science team building custom behavioral models. If you need custom ML models for propensity scoring or next-best-action, you need a data science platform. InfiniSynapse detects behavioral patterns but doesn't replace custom ML development.
  • You've already built a unified behavioral data warehouse. If all your user behavior data is already modeled in a warehouse with a BI layer, you may already have cross-source behavioral capabilities.

InfiniSynapse creates the most value when behavioral questions require joining data across 3+ sources (product + CRM + support + marketing), when cross-source analysis today requires data team tickets, and when product and growth teams need self-service behavioral intelligence.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can InfiniSynapse combine product analytics with CRM and support data?
Yes. This is the core differentiator. Connect product DB, CRM, and support system simultaneously. Ask questions like "do users who submit 3+ support tickets in their first 30 days have lower 90-day retention?" and get cross-source answers.
How is this different from Amplitude or Mixpanel?
Amplitude and Mixpanel excel at in-product behavior tracking. Their limitation: they only see product data. InfiniSynapse complements them by joining product behavior with CRM, support, and billing data for cross-source behavioral questions.
How does InfiniSynapse compare to Hex for behavior analytics?
Hex is strong for data scientists building custom analyses in Python/SQL with a warehouse. InfiniSynapse is for product managers and growth teams asking behavioral questions directly in plain English across all data sources without coding or warehouse prerequisites.
What behavioral analyses can I run?
Retention cohort analysis with behavioral predictors, funnel optimization with drop-off root cause, behavioral user segmentation, churn prediction from activity patterns, and feature adoption impact analysis — all joining data across product, CRM, support, and marketing.
Is user behavior data secure?
Yes. InfiniSynapse supports private deployment (Air-Gapped). All user behavior data, session records, PII, and CRM data stay on your own servers. Essential for companies handling user PII or operating under GDPR and CCPA.
